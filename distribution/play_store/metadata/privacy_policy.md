# Privacy Policy for CoreWar KMP

**Effective Date: December 2024**

CoreWar KMP ("we," "our," or "the App") is a programming simulation game built using Kotlin Multiplatform. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in connection with your use of the App.

## 1. Information Collection
CoreWar KMP is designed with privacy in mind.
- **No Personal Data Collection**: The App does not require user registration. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
- **Local Storage Only**: All game progress, custom "warriors," and settings are stored locally on your device using Android's DataStore and SQLDelight databases. This data is not synchronized to any external servers by us.

## 2. Permissions
The App may request the following permissions only to function correctly:
- **Notifications (Optional)**: To provide updates on battle status or academy progress (if enabled).
- **Storage**: To save and load your custom Redcode scripts on your device.

## 3. Third-Party Services
CoreWar KMP does not include third-party advertising, analytics SDKs, or social media tracking.

## 4. Children's Privacy
CoreWar KMP is a simulation game for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, we are fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the App's documentation and updated version.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the project maintainers via the official GitHub repository.
