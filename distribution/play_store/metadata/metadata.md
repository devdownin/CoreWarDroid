## Full Description (max 4000 chars)
Welcome to CoreWar KMP, the ultimate cyber-battle arena inspired by the classic Core War programming game. Step into a world where code is your only weapon, and your programming skills determine your survival.

In CoreWar KMP, you write powerful "warriors" in Redcode, a low-level assembly-like language. These warriors battle for control over a virtual "Mars" computer, where the goal is to out-think, out-maneuver, and out-execute your opponents.

### Key Features:

*   **Modern Cyber Interface**: Experience the classic battle of code with a sleek, neon-inspired UI optimized for modern Android devices.
*   **Integrated Code Editor**: Write and refine your warriors directly in the app with built-in opcode autocomplete, snippets, and real-time validation.
*   **Deep Strategy**: Master 15 different opcodes and 8 powerful address modes. Develop clever strategies like "Imps", "Dwarfs", and "Vampires".
*   **The Academy**: Unlock and upgrade powerful special abilities as you gain experience. Enhance your warriors with Speed Boosts, Process Shields, and Penalty Reductions.
*   **Chaos Mode**: Test your warriors in an unpredictable environment where memory glitches and process warps can turn the tide of battle at any moment.
*   **Detailed Battle Reports**: Analyze your warrior's performance with in-depth post-game statistics, dominance graphs, and survival metrics.
*   **KMP Powered**: A high-performance core engine built with Kotlin Multiplatform, ensuring a smooth and responsive experience across platforms.
